About Us

Your guide to the best colleges in India

Saarthi.com is a website that helps you find the best colleges for your desired course and exam. Whether you are looking for engineering, MBA, medical, law or design colleges, we have got you covered. We provide you with the most accurate and updated information about colleges, courses, fees, placements, cut-offs, rankings, reviews and more. You can also use our college predictor tool to get a list of colleges that you can get admission in based on your rank, percentile, home state, caste, gender and exam result.

Our mission is to help you make the right choice for your higher education and career. We believe that every student deserves the best guidance and support to achieve their dreams. That is why we have a team of experts and counselors who are always ready to assist you with any queries or doubts. You can also connect with other students and alumni on our platform and get their insights and tips.

Some of the features and benefits of using Saarthi.com are:

  • Access to the most comprehensive and reliable database of colleges in India
  • Easy and user-friendly interface to search, compare and apply to colleges
  • Personalized and data-driven college recommendations based on your preferences and profile
  • Free and accurate college predictor tool to estimate your chances of admission
  • Expert guidance and counseling from our experienced and qualified team
  • Community of students and alumni to share your experiences and learn from others

We hope that you find Saarthi.com useful and helpful in your college search and admission process. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you and improve our services.
